Privacy Policy

Nexus Search is engineered around a single principle: your search is yours alone.

  • ▸ Zero Logs. We do not record, store, or retain any search queries, IP addresses, session data, or browsing history — ever.
  • ▸ No Tracking. There are no cookies for tracking, no analytics scripts, no fingerprinting, and no user profiling of any kind.
  • ▸ No Third-Party Data Sharing. Your queries are never sold, shared, or transmitted to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.
  • ▸ Decentralized Routing. Searches are distributed across multiple independent engines simultaneously so no single source can build a profile on you.
  • ▸ Anonymous by Design. Nexus Search acts as an anonymous proxy between you and search providers. They see the engine, not you.
  • ▸ No Account Required. Full functionality is available with no registration, no sign-in, and no personal information collected.
  • ▸ Preferences Stored Locally. Any optional settings you configure are saved in your browser only, never on our servers.

By using Nexus Search, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy-first operating model.
