Privacy Policy

Nexus Search is engineered around a single principle: your search is yours alone.

  • ▸
    Zero Logs. We do not record, store, or retain any search queries, IP addresses, session data, or browsing history — ever.
  • ▸
    No Tracking. There are no tracking cookies, analytics scripts, fingerprinting, or user profiling of any kind.
  • ▸
    No Third-Party Data Sharing. Your queries are never sold, shared, or transmitted to advertisers or data brokers.
  • ▸
    Decentralized Routing. Searches are distributed across multiple independent engines simultaneously so no single source can profile you.
  • ▸
    Anonymous by Design. Nexus Search acts as an anonymous proxy. Search providers see the engine, not you.
  • ▸
    No Account Required. Full functionality is available with no registration, no sign-in, and no personal information collected.
  • ▸
    Preferences Stored Locally. Any settings you configure are saved in your browser only, never on our servers.

By using Nexus Search, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy-first operating model.
